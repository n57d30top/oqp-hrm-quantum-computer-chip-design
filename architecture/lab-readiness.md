# OQP-HRM Lab Readiness

A software-valid architecture still needs a lab stack before it can become a physical
quantum-computing demonstrator.

Required infrastructure:

- Stable laser at the design wavelength.
- Fiber array or edge-coupler package.
- Polarization control and thermal stabilization.
- Photon-number resolving detector stack, likely cryogenic if SNSPD/TES is used.
- TDC/time tagger.
- Automated phase/loss/source/detector calibration.
- Chip or wafer probing flow.

`oqp lab-readiness` emits the current bench requirements and blockers.
