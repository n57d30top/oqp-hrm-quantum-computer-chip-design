# OQP-HRM Partner Pipeline

Goal: convert the no-budget scorecard into external review evidence and grant leverage.

## Pipeline

- target_list (no cash): Identify 20 academic/foundry/lab reviewers with matching expertise. Success metric: 20 named contacts with one selected review ask each.
- first_outreach (no cash): Send concise partner brief with one narrow artifact review request. Success metric: 5 replies or 2 technical objections.
- review_capture (no cash): Convert replies into written assumption reviews attached to data room. Success metric: 2 signed or attributable technical reviews.
- grant_conversion (no cash): Use reviews to support a foundry-validation grant application. Success metric: 1 submitted grant or partner-funded PDK/S-parameter review.

## Current Assets

- Technical evidence score: `90 / 100`
- Partner diligence readiness: `100 / 100`
- Scorecard completeness: `100 / 100`
- Deterministic yield passing: `True`
